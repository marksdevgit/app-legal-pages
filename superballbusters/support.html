Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I pop balloons?
A: Tap on a balloon to pump it. Each tap increases the balloon's size. When you've pumped enough times (shown by the number), it pops!
Q: What happens if balloons pile up?
A: If 20 balloons accumulate on screen, it's game over! Keep popping to stay safe. The danger music will warn you when you're getting close.
Q: What are the stones?
A: Stones are tough obstacles that require multiple hits to crack. Keep tapping! Some stones contain hidden power-ups inside.
Q: How does Fast Pump Overload work?
A: When Fast Pump is active, there's a 1 in 5 chance that each pump will instantly burst the balloon! It's risky but rewarding.
Q: Does my score get saved?
A: Yes! Your high score is automatically saved locally and submitted to Game Center leaderboards if you're signed in.
Q: How do I turn off sound or haptics?
A: Tap the Settings button on the home screen to toggle:
  • üîä Sound Effects - Toggle game sounds on/off
  • üéµ Music - Toggle background music on/off
  • üì≥ Haptics - Toggle vibration feedback on/off
Your preferences are saved automatically.
Q: Can I compete with friends?
A: Yes! Super Ball Busters integrates with Game Center. Sign in to access leaderboards and compete with players worldwide.
Q: How do I remove ads?
A: You can purchase "Remove Ads" from the Settings screen. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all advertisements. Use "Restore Purchases" to restore on other devices.
Q: What is the Tip Jar?
A: The Tip Jar lets you support development with optional tips. These are completely optional and help us continue improving the game!
Q: I made a purchase but ads are still showing. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  1. Force close the app completely and restart it
  2. Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases"
  3. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase
  4. Restart your device if the issue persists
  5. If still not working, contact support (see below)
Q: The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?
A: Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure your iOS is up to date
  5. If issues persist, contact support (see below)

Tips for Success

  • Use bombs strategically: Pop bombs to clear clusters of nearby balloons!
  • Watch the countdown: Focus on balloons that are almost ready to pop
  • Collect power-ups: They can save you when things get hectic
  • Don't ignore coins: They add up to big bonuses
  • Listen for the warning: Music and haptics warn you when danger is high
  • Use nukes wisely: Save them for when you really need them
