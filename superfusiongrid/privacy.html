Privacy Policy for SuperFusionGrid

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SuperFusionGrid ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

SuperFusionGrid is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

Game Data Stored Locally

  • Game Progress: Your current game state, score, and statistics
  • Settings: Your preferences for music, sound effects, haptics, and quiet mode
  • Custom Palettes: Color palette customizations you create
  • High Scores: Your personal best scores and achievements

All of this data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and is never transmitted to our servers.

Game Center Data

  • Leaderboards: Your scores submitted to Apple's Game Center (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Achievements: Progress toward game achievements (handled entirely by Apple)

Game Center data is managed by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center profile information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

SuperFusionGrid does NOT collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Camera or photo library access
  • Contacts or calendar information

How We Use Your Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress between sessions
  • Remember your settings and preferences
  • Display your statistics and achievements
  • Provide undo functionality during gameplay

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Your game data stays on your device.

The only exception is Game Center data, which you voluntarily submit to Apple's Game Center service and is governed by Apple's privacy policies.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS security features. We do not operate servers or cloud storage for this app.

Children's Privacy

SuperFusionGrid does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete Game Data: Use the app's reset function to clear your game progress
  • Delete All Data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Game Center Data: Manage through your iOS Game Center settings

Future Changes

If we add features that collect or use data differently (such as optional advertisements or cloud sync), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy
  • Make it clear what data is collected
  • Provide opt-in mechanisms where appropriate
  • Notify users through app updates

Third-Party Services

Currently, SuperFusionGrid only uses:

  • Apple Game Center: For leaderboards and achievements (see Apple's Privacy Policy)
